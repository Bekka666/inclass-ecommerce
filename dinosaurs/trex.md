The *Tyrannosaurus* is the **fiercest** dinosaurs that ever existed.

The T-Rex lives in the [Late Cretaceous Period](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cretaceous)

## Features

- Big teeth
- Small arms
- Thick tail
